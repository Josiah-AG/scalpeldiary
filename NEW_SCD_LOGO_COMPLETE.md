# New SCD Logo Design - Complete ✅

## Overview

Created a modern, professional logo featuring the "SCD" acronym (ScalpelDiary) with a medical/surgical theme.

## Logo Design Features

### Visual Elements
- **Blue gradient circles** - Professional medical color scheme
- **Scalpel design element** - Subtle surgical tool representation at the top
- **Bold "SCD" text** - Clear, readable acronym in white
- **Medical cross symbol** - Small cross at the top for medical context
- **Layered design** - Multiple circles with opacity for depth

### Color Scheme
- Primary: `#1e40af` (Deep Blue)
- Secondary: `#2563eb` (Medium Blue)
- Accent: `#60a5fa` (Light Blue)
- Text: `#ffffff` (White)
- Background (app icon): `#000000` (Black)
